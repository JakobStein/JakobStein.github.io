I'm an EPSRC Postdoctoral Research Associate at University College London, having recently finished my doctorate under Lorenzo Foscolo at the same institution. My research interests are in applications of cohomogeneity-one symmetry methods, geometric analysis, and the study gauge theory on Riemannian manifolds with special holonomy. I am currently working with Matt Turner (Bath) on constructions of G2-instantons on cohomogeniety-one G2-metrics, and with Daniel Fadel (Brest Occidental) on a gluing construction for G2-instantons on non-compact manifolds with isolated conical singularities.
