I'm an EPSRC Postdoctoral Research Associate at University College London, having finished my doctorate under Lorenzo Foscolo at the same institution. My research interests include, but are not limited to: geometric analysis, gauge theory and Riemannian manifolds with special holonomy groups. I am currently working with Matt Turner (University of Bath) on constructions of G2-instantons on cohomogeniety-one G2-metrics, and with Daniel Fadel (IMPA) on a gluing construction for G2-instantons on non-compact manifolds with isolated conical singularities.
